## Compatibility with Modern Pose Renderers

The representation is designed to connect naturally with modern pose-conditioned renderers. In the accompanying qualitative examples, the same DWPose sequence can be fed into multiple rendering systems while preserving coarse motion structure and temporal progression.

![Qualitative comparison of pose-conditioned sign video rendering](./static/images/qualitative_grid.jpg)

- The examples include both commercial and open-source renderers under a shared pose input.
- The point of this layer is not to define a final video benchmark yet, but to show that the released representation is already aligned with contemporary pose-to-video workflows.
